I don't know when the thought emerged, but it is clear: I hoped to become her desk mate. Due to Qi Haozheng ‘ s interference, she didn’t have the opportunity to choose, which resulted in that expectation remaining in my heart. My expectation was not particularly strong—just strong enough to exist.

It was not until today that everything was reversed. My wish finally came true. I had always been waiting for that day.
