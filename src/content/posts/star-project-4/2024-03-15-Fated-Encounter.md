It was in spring when I met her. Bathed in smooth wind,I was walking randomly along the road by the lake. Then she emerged on the opposite bank of the lake,talking and laughing with her friends. Although there was a long distance between us,I could notice her unbelievable beauty,which was hard to describe. We were facing toward the same direction,so we were approaching each other. A few minutes later,we met on the dam to the south of the lake. I glanced in her shiny eyes,only to find shyness in them. With delight welling up from the bottom of my heart,I turned my head aside,staring at the middle of the lake. I seemed to be able to feel the scent smell around her,even after a long time.
